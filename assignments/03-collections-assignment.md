# 3-Modul Topshiriqlari: Collections va DSA

## Topshiriqlar

### 1. ArrayList bilan ishlash
ArrayList yarating va unda CRUD amallarini bajaring.

### 2. HashMap bilan ishlash
Talabalar bazasini HashMap da saqlang (ID → Talaba).

### 3. Stack va Queue
Stack va Queue yordamida amaliy masalalarni yeching.

### 4. Custom Iterator
O'zingizning Iterator sinfingizni yarating.

### 5. Comparator
Talabalarni GPA bo'yicha saralash uchun Comparator yarating.
